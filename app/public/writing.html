"HOLLOW"

I like the feeling of hollow.

The emptiness
In-between the collarbone.

Show me some spine.
Give me some ribs.

Spread your thighs.

Best if they don’t touch.

The gap is supposed
To be
Beautiful—

So I like the feeling of hollow.



"CITIES"

People
Collapsing the streets—
Distorted faces,
Edvard Munch paints
Them against
The reflective skyscrapers.

Although the city
Still breathes,
The minds of millions
Suffocate the streets—
Thought bubbles like umbrellas
Dot the boulevard.


Trees
Pumping oxygen into
The industrial landscape,
The minority provides
A crucial backbone,
The forests and trees web
A network
In-between concrete
And plexiglass.


Each face impassive,
The ability
To separate,
To disengage,
And hide in its turtle shell
Of a skull.
