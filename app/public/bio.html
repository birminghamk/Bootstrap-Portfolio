So... what is dopamine?

Dopamine is a neurotransmitter in the brain, mostly known for the feeling of pleasure and seeking rewards.

Utilizing my passions for art, science, and writing, I aim to create things that make you feel that dopaminergic surge.

Is that the only thing dopamine does?

Certainly not!

Stay tuned for an illustrated animation on what some dopamine in your brain can do, And what can happen if there isn't enough!

Basically, the brain is cool. Let's see what we can make with it.
