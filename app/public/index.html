Masters Gallery

The Masters Gallery website needed a face-lift, not only to enhance the visual appeal, but to focus all the attention where it should be–

ON THE ARTISTS.

Utilizing a black and white color scheme, I added stylistic and content upgrades, creating a unified, sleek theme that captures the brand, and directs the user's eye to the artists, and their bodies of work.

In addition to stylistic changes, there was a lack of content spanning major sections of the website, including the 'About Us' and 'Exhibitions' pages...

I added engaging and informative content, highlighting the gallery's stunning interior and decades of experience.

The website makeover was complete, but it needed to be consistent; using CSS Media Queries, I made the site responsive across all screen sizes.

Welcome to dopamineCream!

So... what is dopamine?

Dopamine is a neurotransmitter (chemical messenger) in the brain, mostly known for the feeling of pleasure and seeking rewards.

Utilizing my passions for art, science, and writing, I aim to create things that make you feel like you are taking a deep breath whilst laying in a hammock in the mountains.

I enjoy creating and learning new things with a whimsical yet serene style, inspired by my city and the outdoorsy culture of Denver, CO.
